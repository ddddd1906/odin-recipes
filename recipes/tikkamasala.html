Description

Chicken tikka masala is a popular Indian dish made with marinated chicken cooked in a rich, spiced sauce.

The dish is known for its creamy texture and aromatic spices, making it a favorite among many.

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast
  • Yogurt
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Tomato sauce
  • Heavy cream
  • Garam masala
  • Cumin
  • Coriander
  • Turmeric
  • Chili powder
  • Salt
  • Vegetable oil

Steps

  1. Marinate the chicken in yogurt, garlic, ginger, and spices for at least 1 hour.
  2. Grill or bake the marinated chicken until cooked through.
  3. In a pan, heat oil and sauté garlic and ginger until fragrant.
  4. Add tomato sauce and spices, and cook for a few minutes.
  5. Stir in heavy cream and simmer until the sauce thickens.
  6. Add the cooked chicken to the sauce and simmer for a few more minutes.
  7. Serve hot with rice or naan bread.
